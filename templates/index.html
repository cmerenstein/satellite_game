Welcome to Satellite Sam!


The point of the game is to figure out what city you've been dropped into, using only an unlabled view of google earth. Your goal is to get as close as possible, zoom in and look around for clues to at least guess what part of the world you're in. Use the map below the image to enter your guess for the city's location, and make sure to scroll all the way down and hit submit. Good luck!
